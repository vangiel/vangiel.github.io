International conference WAF 2020

The 21st International Workshop of Physical Agents (WAF) was suppoused to be held at the Rectorado of the Universitdad de Alcalá in Alcalá de Henares, Madrid, Spain. However, due to the current COVID-19 pandemic it will be online

International conference RO-MAN 2020

This conference on Robot & Human Interactive Communication was expected to be held in Napoles. However, due to the COVID-19 pandemic situation, it will be hosted online and for free for everyone who wants to attend.
